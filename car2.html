The Lamborghini Aventador is a flagship supercar known for its aggressive design and incredible power. It’s equipped with a naturally aspirated 6.5-liter V12 engine that produces over 730 horsepower. The Aventador can accelerate from 0–100 km/h in just 2.9 seconds and reach a top speed of 350 km/h. With its signature scissor doors and aerodynamically sculpted body, the Aventador perfectly blends performance, luxury, and Italian craftsmanship.
